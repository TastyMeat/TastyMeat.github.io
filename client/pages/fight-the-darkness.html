Fight The Darkness

 Next →

What a wonderful nap... wait, where am I? Awakening from your peaceful slumber, you find yourself in a mysterious cave. Your curiosity leads you to explore the cave ultimately stumbling upon an ancient evil, THE CORRUPTION. It's spreading... it's coming towards you! With your trusty pistol and reliable pickaxe (don't ask why you carry these), stop the approaching darkness... However, a small voice in your head tells you that it is inevitable, the CORRUPTION SHALL CONSUME.

Controls:

  • Movement - WASD
  • Aim - Mouse
  • Melee Attack - Left Click
  • Ranged Attack - Right Click
  • Pickup/Swap Weapon - E

Side note: The progress bar above indicates the area coverage of the darkness, destroy darkness cells to slow it down!

Have FUN!
